Hey Climber, here's a Bit About Classical Conditioning

Classical Conditioning's Coach

Dylan Barks has been pursuing his passion for rock climbing since he was 12 years old. Now he is looking forward to sharing that passion with the climbing community through online coaching.

Growing up in Ann Arbor, Michigan, Dylan found hismself a long way from the nearest climbing. However, with the discovery of the Red River Gorge in Kentucky, he was able to hone his skills through numerous weekend trips to The Red. The RRG is still where his hardest ascent is to this day, the notorious Southern Smoke Direct (5.14d).

Dylan has won numerous competitions and has many notable top fisnishes. In 2018 he placed fifth at the Bouldering National Championships. He came away with the same result at the Sport Climbing National Championships.

Dylan has been coaching for over six years, first adapting his skill into a teachable form at Planet Rock, his home gym in Ann Arbor, Michigan. He sharpened his coaching skills with the Planet Rock Team, being able to send multiple athletes to the Youth World Championships. An athlete of his was also crowned Pan American Champion. He is very excited to take this experience and break into the realm of online training!
